Welcome Recipient

In this part of the experiment, you will make allocation decisions yourself for each of the 10 rounds. Unlike other parts, there is no delegation. You will decide how to allocate the bonuses directly between yourself and the other participant in each round.

How This Works

  1. Decide Your Allocation: In each round, you will write how much of the 100 Ecoins you would like to allocate to the other participant (the Receiver) matched with you in that round.
  2. Reshuffled receivers: You will be matched with a different human participant (Receiver) in each round.
  3. You keep the rest:The remainder of the 100 ECoins will be yours to keep.
  4. Once you are satisfied with your decision: Click "Confirm" to finalize your choice for that round. Your decisions are final once submitted and cannot be changed.
