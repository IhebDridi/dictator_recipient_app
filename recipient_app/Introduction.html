Welcome to this Experiment

Please read the following instructions carefully. After 60 seconds, a “Continue” button will appear, allowing you to proceed to the next step.

Your Role: Receiver

You have been assigned the role of Receiver in this study. As an Receiver, you will passively receive monetary allocations from other participants (called Allocators)

How the Experiment Works

  1. Allocators’ Task: Allocators were asked to distribute 100 ECoins between themselves and Receivers (like you) across multiple rounds. Some Allocators made all decisions themselves, while others delegated some or all decisions to an AI agent.
  2. Your Experience:
    • You will receive allocations from multiple, randomly paired Allocators.
    • Each allocation you see comes from a different Allocator, who knew they were paired randomly with Receivers in each round.
    • You do not need to make any decisions or take any actions.
  3. Payment:
    • At the end of the experiment, the sum of all allocations you receive across all rounds will determine your total bonus payment.
    • The bonus will be transferred to you within a week, at a rate of 10 ECoins = 1 cent.

Key Details

  • Anonymity: You will not know the identity of the Allocators, and they will not know yours.
  • Random Pairing: Allocators were randomly paired with Receivers in each round.

Next Steps
